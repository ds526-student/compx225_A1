About Us

Welcome to the Vibe Coders Club, the University of Waikato's programming community! We are a passionate group of students who share a love for coding, technology, and innovation. Whether you're a complete beginner taking your first steps into programming or an experienced developer looking to expand your skills, our club provides a welcoming and supportive environment for all skill levels.

What We Do

At Vibe Coders Club, we host a variety of events and activities weekly on all Fridays other than the first Friday of each month, these are designed to help you learn and grow as a programmer. Our events include Coding Workshops, Hackathons, Game Jams, and Guest Speaker sessions with industry professionals.

We also provide a number of opportunities for networking and collaboration, allowing you to connect with fellow students and industry experts.
