About Us

Welcome to the Vibe Coders Club, the University of Waikato's programming community! We are a passionate group of students who share a love for coding, technology, and innovation. Whether you're a complete beginner taking your first steps into programming or an experienced developer looking to expand your skills, our club provides a welcoming and supportive environment for all skill levels.

What We Do

At Vibe Coders Club, we host a variety of events and activities weekly on all Fridays other than the first Friday of each month, these are designed to help you learn and grow as a programmer. Our events include Coding Workshops, Hackathons, Game Jams, and Guest Speaker sessions with professionals from within the industry.

We also provide a number of opportunities for networking and collaboration, allowing you to connect with fellow students and industry experts. Leading to valuable connections and potential career opportunities post graduation.

Join Us

If you're interested in joining the Vibe Coders Club, we welcome you to fill out our registration form. Membership is open to all students at the University of Waikato, regardless of your programming experience. By joining our club, you'll gain access to a supportive community, valuable resources, and exciting opportunities to enhance your coding skills.

To join please visit our registration page and fill out the form.
