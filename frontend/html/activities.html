Activities

Python Programming Workshop - 18/07/25

Today we had a workshop on Python programming.

For those new to the language, we covered the basics of Python including syntax, data types, and control structures. For the more experienced, we explored pygame for game development as we learnt to create a mini top down shooter game. It was a great opportunity for everyone to learn and share knowledge about Python programming.

Guest Speaker - 25/07/25

Today we had a guest speaker, Jonathan Rogers, technical director at Grinding Gear Games located in Auckland. As one of the founders of the company, he shared his journey in the gaming industry and provided insights into game development. He discussed the challenges and successes he has faced throughout his career, and provided valuable advice for those interested in pursuing a career in game development.

It was an inspiring session that motivated many of us to continue pursuing our passion for programming and game development.

Hackathon - 01/08/25

This was a part of our bi-annual hackathon event where we had pairs of students compete to solve the most problems over a 3 day period. The event was a great success, with many students participating and showcasing their skills in problem-solving and coding.

The hackathon provided an opportunity for students to collaborate, learn from each other, and push their limits in a competitive yet friendly environment.

Upcoming Workshops

Godot Programming Workshop - 15/08/25

Join us for a workshop in the Godot game engine, where we will learn to create a simple 2D platformer. Doing this we will be covering the basics of Godot, including its scene system, scripting with GDScript, and creating interactive game elements. This workshop is perfect for those new to game development or looking to expand their skills in Godot.

For those who are more experienced, we will be exploring advanced topics such as physics, animations, and enemy/npc AI in Godot.

Web Development Workshop - 22/08/25

In this workshop, we will be diving into web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We will cover the basics of creating a responsive website, including layout design, styling with CSS, and adding interactivity with JavaScript. This workshop is ideal for those looking to get started in web development or enhance their existing skills.

For those with more experience, we will be exploring advanced topics such as responsive design, animations, and integrating APIs into web applications.

Game Jam - 29/08/25

Join us for our monthly game jam, where we will provide a theme and participants will have 48 hours to create a game based on that theme. This is a great opportunity to collaborate with others, learn new skills, and showcase your creativity in game development. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, everyone is welcome to participate and have fun!

The game jam will be held over the weekend, starting on Friday evening and ending on Sunday evening.
